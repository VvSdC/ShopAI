## Why ShopAI?

Traditional online stores make you click through filters, menus, and checkout steps. **ShopAI puts shopping in a conversation.**

Whether you want a cricket ball, need to track an order, or are ready to pay — just ask. Our AI assistant understands what you mean, pulls real product and order data, and guides you from discovery to delivery. No hunting through pages. No guesswork.

**ShopAI is for people who want shopping to feel fast, personal, and effortless.**

---

## What You Get

### Shop with AI

Your built-in shopping companion — not a generic chatbot.

- **Find products in plain language** — “Show me cricket gear under ₹2000” or “Something for a birthday gift”
- **Full-page assistant** with saved conversation history, plus a quick chat widget while you browse
- **Cart & checkout from chat** — add items, update quantities, apply coupons, and pay without leaving the conversation
- **Order tracking & account help** — check order status, shipping addresses, and active promotions
- **Stays on topic** — focused on shopping, orders, and your account — with clear AI disclosure at every step
- **Transparent by design** — you always know you’re talking to an AI assistant, with clear guidance to verify prices before you pay

### A Store Worth Browsing

Everything you expect from a modern shop — polished and ready to use.

- **Curated catalog** across categories, brands, colors, and sizes — fast to load even as your catalog grows
- **Smart search & filters** — keyword plus meaning-based hybrid search, reranked so the best matches rise to the top
- **Trending picks & category highlights** on the homepage
- **Rich product pages** with multiple images, live stock info, and customer reviews
- **Persistent cart** that stays in sync when you’re signed in (including when the assistant updates it)

### Deals That Actually Work

- **Active promotions** surfaced on the homepage and in chat
- **Coupon codes** applied at cart or checkout — validated on the server so discounts are real
- **Seasonal offers** on trending products across categories

### Checkout You Can Trust

- **Secure payments** powered by Stripe
- **Prices enforced on the server** — what you see is what you pay
- **Stock reserved atomically when you pay** — no overselling; cancelled orders release inventory correctly
- **Checkout that recovers** — if you close the tab, payment polling and background jobs still reconcile your order
- **Order updates** from placement through dispatch and delivery
- **Email confirmations** when you order (with resend if needed)

### Reviews You Can Rely On

- **AI-powered moderation** keeps toxic, spammy, or unsafe content out of your feed
- **Helpful review tags** — like “fast delivery” or “great value” — so you can scan what matters in seconds
- **Smarter product discovery** — new items are tagged automatically so search and the assistant understand them better over time

### Your Account, Your Way

- **Sign up & sign in** with a smooth, secure experience
- **Password reset via email OTP** — no broken links, works everywhere
- **Profile & multiple shipping addresses** — add, edit, or remove as you need
- **Order history** with payment and fulfillment status at a glance

### For Store Owners & Admins

Run the business from one dashboard:

- **Products, categories, brands & inventory** — full control over what you sell
- **Orders & customers** — update fulfillment, cancel with automatic refunds where policy allows, view sales
- **Coupons & promotions** — create time-bound offers; expiry syncs to storefront and assistant automatically
- **Sales insights** — totals, trends, and order value at a glance
- **Developer Analytics** — smoke-test AI providers, run chat eval suites, and inspect token usage and routing in production

## How It Works

**1. Browse or ask**  
Explore categories and trending products — or open **Shop with AI** and describe what you need.

**2. Build your cart**  
Add items from product pages or tell the assistant what to put in your cart. Apply coupons when they’re available.

**3. Checkout & track**  
Pay securely, get email confirmation, and ask the assistant anytime about your order status.
